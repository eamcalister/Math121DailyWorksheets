    Handout 11.3 Friday Week 11 - Power Functions
    

    Objectives

    • Define and recognize power functions.
      🔗
      🔗
    • Find formulas for power functions from points on the graph.
      🔗
      🔗
    • Identify general graph features based on the coefficient and exponent in power functions.
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗
    Power functions are one more basic class of functions that involves two parameters. They are relatively simple to work with algebraically, but in contrast to other families, they can display a multitude of graphical behaviors.
